Public Pasta Drop-In

Our Thursday community pasta night in Edmonton. Two seatings, fresh pasta, a seat at the family table.

What is the Public Pasta Drop-In?

The Public Pasta Drop-In is da Cecot's weekly Thursday-evening pasta night in Edmonton. Two seatings — 6–7 PM and 7–8 PM, $45 per person. Choose your pasta and sauce from the bar, sit down with the neighbourhood, and enjoy fresh Italian comfort food the way we'd serve it at home.

What to expect
